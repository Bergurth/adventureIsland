It is a beautiful Sunday afternoon. The sun is shining. You are having a family picnic. Your mother, June, and her sister Jane are sitting on a blanket, talking.

Your father, Paul Smith, is feeding the ducks by the pond with your sister Jane. Your brother, Jason, and your two cousins are playing soccer. Jason’s dog is running around barking and chasing the ball.

There’s a bench by the pond. Your uncle Bob is sitting on the bench reading the newspaper. There‘s a cute little rabbit under the bench. Your sister, Jane, is taking pictures of the rabbit with her new smartphone.

Your grandparents are also coming to the picnic and you see them walking down the path. They are a little late. They are carrying a picnic basket together. It seems a little heavy. You run to help them.
