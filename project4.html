You walkhome, excited after a fun day at the park. You can’t believe the good news! “Wow! Australia! And three weeks cruising on a ship! My friends will be so jealous!”

You come to your neighborhood. You can see the apartment blocks in the background and some houses in the front surrounded by gardens. You see a man mowing the lawn in his garden and a boy sitting on the sidewalk, crying. You wonder why he is so upset. Then you see; he dropped his ice-cream cone on the sidewalk.

In the next garden there is a girl, as happy as a pig in mud, swinging in a swing tied to a tree branch. She’s laughing and waving to you. You know her, she goes to your school. You wave and shout happily, “Hi Sandra, I am going to Australia!” as you skip by.
